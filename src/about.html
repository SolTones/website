About Us

SolTones is a 2,000 piece collection built upon the Solana Blockchain. Founded in September 2021 by 4 friends looking to leave the Solana NFT metaverse better than we found it.

The NFT space is fundamentally built upon decentralized technology. Yet, it quickly became apparent that most projects have made the conscious decision to construct specifically centralized projects. SolTones decided to break from this trend and create a project that is making it a priority to enable, support, and exemplify cross-project integrations.

SolTones, at its most basic, is color ownership provided through 2,000 NFTs. Color is a foundational and fundamental building block of all NFTs. It thus provides us with the flexibility necessary to integrate with a broad range of projects. The SolTones team also felt it was inappropriate to charge people for such a primitive component of the NFT metaverse. Thus the decision was made to airdrop the entire collection, for free, to our community.

Each SolTone has its RGB hex code as part of the image, and a limited subset is given English names. The color information for each SolTone is also included within the metadata of each NFT, making it easy to extract color information for use in integrations and collaborations. The team is also working on standardizing how color information is stored within an NFT and developing a technical standard that defines how projects can relay color information for integrations. On the flip side, the team is also developing open-source frameworks and tools to aid teams in integrating with projects that provide color information.

We are incredibly excited about what the future of cross-project integrations and collaborations looks like. We hope you enjoy us on this journey!
