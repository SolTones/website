Three-Prong Approach

SolTones is determined to become a driving force pushing for cross-project deep integrations. There are three primary areas of focus for the SolTones project.

Read more about our progress towards these goals on our integrations page.

#000001

Mint-Time Customization

Enable SolTone owners to influence the creation of any NFT at the time of mint.

#000002

Post-Mint Customization

Enable SolTone owners to modify any of their NFTs by utilizing the SolTones they own.

#000003

Ephemeral Customization

Temporary or superficial customizations with any NFT project, website, or game.
