Partner Integrations

The NFT space is fundamentally built upon decentralized technology. Yet, the large majority of today's projects have made the conscious decision to construct specifically centralized projects. The irony of the situation has not been lost on the SolTones team and community. In contrast with the norm, the SolTones project is building one of the fundamental building blocks of the Solana NFT metaverse; color ownership. SolTones is taking a different approach by finding like-minded projects that share our truly decentralized project landscape vision. We can build some of the first Deep Integrations within the Solana ecosystem by partnering with these projects.

If you are interested in exploring ways to enable your users to influence your project through color ownership, do not hesitate to reach out!

Aiternate Universe

Aiternate Universe is a collection of 555 3D entities algorithmically generated. Through their website, holders of their collections can view and interact with the NFTs in a genuinely novel way: by utilizing a custom-built web viewer and explorer. Aiternate Universe has also integrated SolTones; users can influence the color of their Aiternate Universe entities based upon the color SolTones that they own. The partnership with Aiternate Universe exemplifies how 2 projects can work together to bring value to both of their communities by providing temporary changes to the way an NFT is viewed.

SHAPES x SolTones

The SHAPES projects are comprised of art that is built through recursively generated primitives. Each forged piece of art is created on a massive 10,000 x 10,000-pixel canvas to fully take advantage of the recursive nature of generation. The SHAPES x SolTones collaborative collection is a first of its kind on Solana. The SHAPES x SolTones collection allows people to influence the NFTs they mint by utilizing the color SolTones they hold in their wallets at the time of mint. This project allowed us to explore and push the boundaries of what is possible to achieve regarding cross-project collaborations at mint-time.

Parametrise ToneBox

Parametrise has built a name by releasing limited series collections built around proprietary "boxing" software. This "boxing" software can take any piece of art, decompose it into its foundational colors, and then realize the work into 3D boxes comprising the underlying colors. The Parametrise and SolTones teams knew there was a natural harmony between projects. Thus, a collaborative series was born; the ToneBox series. ToneBox is a limited series of 125 pieces algorithmically produced by combining tools and processes developed by both projects. 125 of the most famous fine art pieces were taken and run through software developed by the SolTones team to reimagine the artworks as mosaics of SolTones. These reimagined pieces were handed off to the Parametrise team that ran these mosaics through their "boxing" software to generate the final items of the collection. This process resulted in a series of generative art pieces that exemplify the best of both projects.
